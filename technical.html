Technical Specification

Statistical methodology and empirical validation for multivariate structural deviation detection.

Methodology

Baseline Construction

30 cycle baseline window. Mean vector and covariance matrix computed per engine. Covariance stability validated prior to drift computation.

Drift Detection

Mahalanobis distance applied to standardized sensor channels. 7 cycle smoothing. 2 hit logic. 4 sigma threshold.

Empirical Validation

NASA CMAPSS FD001 through FD004 degradation trajectories. 707 engines total. 96.9 percent detection coverage. Median 183 cycle lead time. Zero baseline window admissions.

FD004 engine 190 example. Admission at cycle 36. Failure at cycle 243. Lead 207 cycles.
