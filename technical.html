Technical Specification

System Boundary, Analytical Framework, and Empirical Validation

Neraium operates as a read only infrastructure intelligence layer. It ingests telemetry, performs multivariate deviation modeling, and produces structured analytical records. The platform does not execute control actions or modify system state.

01 System Boundary
Operational Scope
The platform receives native telemetry and produces analytical outputs. It does not manage equipment, alter control states, or perform automated remediation.

Read Only Telemetry Ingestion

Signals are received passively without issuing downstream commands.

Multivariate Drift Modeling

Deviation is computed across correlated parameters relative to established baselines.

Structured Evidence Output

Analytical state is preserved for operator review and governance documentation.

02 Methodology
Benchmark Analytical Framework
Validation utilized 30 cycle baseline modeling, Mahalanobis multivariate drift computation, 7 cycle smoothing, 2 hit admission logic, and 4 sigma threshold determination.
