Layered Architecture

Our technical architecture decouples ingestion, analysis, and review to preserve system boundaries and ensure evidence integrity. Each layer has a clear purpose and audit trail.

Layer 01
Ingest
Protocol adapters handle data from BAS, SCADA, and IoT sensors. Signals are normalized and validated to ensure downstream integrity.
→
Layer 02
Analyze
Our multi-signal engine correlates and scores deviation trajectories across domains. Operators and investors can trace results back to raw inputs.
→
Layer 03
Review
Insights and records are preserved immutably, enabling operators to act and investors to evaluate with confidence.
