Governance

Operational principles and infrastructure transparency

Neraium operates as a read-only intelligence layer that provides system visibility without modifying control systems or replacing operator authority.

Principles

Governance by design

The platform is structured to preserve system boundaries, maintain auditability, and keep intelligence subordinate to human decision-making.

Read-only architecture

The platform observes infrastructure conditions without issuing commands or changing state.

Operational independence

Existing control systems remain authoritative in the operating environment.

Transparency

Infrastructure conditions become more measurable, interpretable, and reviewable.

Accountability

Operational outputs remain traceable through preserved logic and system records.

Authority boundaries

Operators remain the decision point

Neraium helps people understand infrastructure behaviour. It does not replace the operator, assume control authority, or intervene directly in the operating environment.

Infrastructure systems

Continue generating telemetry and maintaining control authority over assets and operations.

Neraium layer

Interprets telemetry and preserves structured operational intelligence outputs.

Human decision-making

Action remains external to the platform and within existing operational structures.
