Governance

Neraium operates as a read-only infrastructure intelligence layer designed to provide system visibility while preserving operational autonomy.

Operational Principles

Infrastructure systems operate under strict operational requirements. Monitoring platforms must therefore provide insight without interfering with system control environments.

Governance Framework

Read Only Architecture

Neraium observes telemetry environments without controlling equipment.

Operational Independence

Existing control systems remain unchanged.

Infrastructure Transparency

Operators gain clear visibility into system behavior.

Accountability

Operational intelligence remains transparent and auditable.

Institutional Alignment

Infrastructure operators require systems that enhance operational awareness without introducing new operational risks.

By operating as a read-only intelligence layer, Neraium provides insight while maintaining strict operational independence from control systems.
