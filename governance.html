Operational Governance // ALETHEIA GATE

The Deterministic Filter.

Neraium does not use black-box heuristics. Every alert is passed through **Aletheia's Gate**, a rules-based engine that cross-references telemetry against the laws of thermodynamics and historical baselines.

01 / Verification

Signals are validated against secondary and tertiary sensors to eliminate ghost-triggers.

02 / Context

Local environmental variables are correlated to ensure drift is systemic, not seasonal.

03 / Authority

All intelligence is signed and traceable, creating a defensible chain of institutional record.
