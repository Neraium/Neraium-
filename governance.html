Governance

Operational principles for a read-only intelligence layer

Neraium provides infrastructure visibility without modifying operational control. Authority remains with existing systems and human operators.

Architecture Read-only
Control No actuation
Decision Model Operator-led
Accountability Auditable
Principles

Designed to support operators, not replace them

Infrastructure intelligence should improve visibility without altering the authority structure of operational systems. The Neraium platform is built around this boundary.

Read-Only Observation

The platform receives telemetry signals but does not issue commands or modify control system behavior.

Operational Independence

Existing SCADA, control logic, and automation layers remain unchanged and continue to govern operations.

Infrastructure Transparency

Operators gain a coherent view of infrastructure behavior across multiple telemetry sources.

Accountability

All surfaced intelligence is preserved as evidence so that interpretations remain explainable and reviewable.
