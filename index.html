Operational Boundary

Neraium is a read-only infrastructure intelligence layer. It does not actuate, control, modify, or override mechanical or water systems. It observes telemetry, classifies sustained structural deviation, and presents structured intelligence for operator review.

All operational authority remains with licensed operators and infrastructure owners.

02

System Parameters

Infrastructure failure begins with correlated drift. Pressure instability, flow irregularity, pump efficiency decay, vibration escalation, chemical imbalance, and thermal divergence accumulate long before terminal events.

Neraium evaluates these signals continuously across the full telemetry environment as a correlated system rather than isolated alerts.

Applicable to municipal water distribution, industrial process systems, pump stations, district cooling networks, commercial mechanical infrastructure, and managed facilities operating live telemetry.
