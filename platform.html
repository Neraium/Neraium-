The Neraium Platform

The platform transforms infrastructure telemetry into operational intelligence by analyzing system relationships across operational environments.

Platform Architecture

The Neraium platform operates as an intelligence layer that analyzes telemetry data from infrastructure systems without modifying operational controls.

Core Components

Telemetry Ingestion

Operational telemetry streams are collected from infrastructure systems.

System Modeling

Infrastructure environments are mapped as operational systems.

Pattern Detection

Operational Signals

The platform operates alongside existing infrastructure systems without requiring changes to operational control environments.

Advantages

Early Signal Detection

Deviation patterns are detected long before failure occurs.

Operational Transparency

Operators gain system level visibility.

System Independence

Existing operational systems remain unchanged.

Scalable Analysis

The system can analyze telemetry across large environments.
