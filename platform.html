Platform

A read-only intelligence layer for industrial infrastructure

Neraium analyzes existing plant telemetry to identify structural changes in system behavior before failures occur, without interacting with control systems or modifying plant operations.

How It Works

Built to operate alongside existing monitoring infrastructure

The system reads existing sensor and process data but does not interact with control systems or change plant operations. This makes deployment low risk and allows Neraium to run alongside existing monitoring infrastructure.

By analyzing relationships across sensors and equipment, the platform detects changes in system behavior that may indicate emerging degradation or instability.

Platform Structure

Three parts of the platform

Telemetry intake

Ingests plant telemetry from existing sensor and process data sources without requiring workflow replacement.

System analysis

Evaluates how signals across an industrial system interact and how those relationships shift over time.

Actionable output

Highlights equipment or processes that may require attention so teams can review developing issues earlier.

Where This Applies

Designed for complex industrial environments

  • Steel production and melt shops
  • Heavy manufacturing facilities
  • Energy and power infrastructure
  • Large mechanical and thermal systems

In these environments, early signals of instability often appear across multiple signals before problems become visible in individual measurements.
