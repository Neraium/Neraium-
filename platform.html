Platform

A platform built for defensible infrastructure interpretation

Neraium separates signal detection, interpretation governance, and evidentiary accountability so signals remain useful under operational, engineering, and regulatory scrutiny.

1

Signal Geometry Layer

Detects system level drift from multivariate telemetry without relying on large historical failure datasets.

2

Aletheia's Gate

Applies deterministic rules for temporal persistence, physical interpretation, and cross signal coherence before escalation.

3

Evidence Vault Protocol

Preserves a verifiable record of system divergence, evaluation criteria, system state, and final interpretation outcome.

Operational role

Neraium operates as a read only interpretation layer alongside existing monitoring and control environments. It does not control equipment or issue directives.

Deployment

The platform is designed for infrastructure edge deployment and can process telemetry locally without depending on continuous cloud connectivity.
