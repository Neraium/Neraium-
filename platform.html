The Neraium Platform

The Neraium platform transforms operational telemetry into infrastructure intelligence. Instead of relying on reactive alarms, the system analyzes entire telemetry environments to detect structural system drift before operational failure occurs.

Platform Overview

Modern infrastructure environments generate large volumes of telemetry data through sensors, control systems, and monitoring platforms. While these systems provide valuable operational insight, most monitoring tools analyze individual metrics rather than system relationships.

Neraium analyzes telemetry environments as complete operational systems, identifying structural changes that occur across multiple variables.

Platform Components

Telemetry Ingestion

Operational telemetry is ingested from infrastructure systems including pumps, heat exchangers, cooling systems, manufacturing equipment, and facility telemetry.

System Modeling

Infrastructure environments are mapped as interconnected operational systems rather than isolated assets.

Multivariate Analysis

The platform analyzes relationships between telemetry variables to detect subtle changes in system behavior.

Intelligence Output

Operators receive early visibility into deterioration patterns and system drift.

Infrastructure Environments

The Neraium platform is designed to operate across infrastructure environments where telemetry systems already exist.

Industrial Operations

Manufacturing systems generate telemetry from pumps, motors, conveyors, and process equipment.

Cooling Infrastructure

Large scale cooling environments including towers, exchangers, and thermal systems.

Facility Operations

Building infrastructure systems including mechanical and water distribution environments.

Operational Networks

Telemetry networks across distributed infrastructure systems.

Operational Advantages

Earlier Detection

Deviation patterns can be detected weeks before failure events.

Reduced Downtime

Operators gain earlier insight into deterioration patterns.

Improved Maintenance Planning

Maintenance can be scheduled before emergencies occur.

System Visibility

Infrastructure networks become transparent and measurable.
