About Craig Curtis

Craig Curtis is the founder of Neraium and the creator of Systemic Infrastructure Intelligence, a platform designed to detect instability and failure risk in complex infrastructure systems.

His work focuses on identifying structural relationships between sensors across machines and industrial environments to reveal early signals of degradation that traditional monitoring systems cannot detect.
